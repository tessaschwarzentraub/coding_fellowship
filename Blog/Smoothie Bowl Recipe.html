Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Ingredients for 1:

  • 1 cup frozen fruit
  • 3/4 cup apple juice
  • Granola
  • wresh fruit and dried fruit of choice
  • walnuts (optional)
  • shredded coconut

Blend frozen fruit and apple juice thoroughly, if consistency is too thick, add more apple juice as needed

Transfer smoothie mixture to a bowl

Sprinkle all remaining ingredients onto the smoothie mixture...and enjoy!
