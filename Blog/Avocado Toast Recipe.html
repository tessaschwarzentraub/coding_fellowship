Avocado Toast Recipe

Ingreidents for 1:

  • avocado
  • lemon
  • sourdough Bread
  • 1 egg
  • chia, flax, and hemp seeds
  • garlic powder
  • salt and pepper

Cook egg in boiling water for 8 minutes with a pinch of salt

Toast sourdough bread

Mash avocado with fork Combine avocado, lemon, garlic powder, salt, and pepper

Spread avocado combination on toasted bread

Peel hardboiled Egg and half before placing on the avocado toast

Sprinkle chia, flax, and hemp seed on top....and enjoy!
