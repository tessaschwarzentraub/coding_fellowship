Oatmeal Recipie

Ingredients for 1:

  • 1/2 cup steel cut oats
  • 1/2 cup water or milk of choice
  • tbs. maple syrup
  • 2 tbs. coconut sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • chia, flax, and hemp seeds
  • 1tbs. nut butter
  • 1 banana
  • handful dried cranberries
  • handful pecans

Combine oats and water or milk in small pan

Heat over medium heat until mixture has thickened and moisture is gone

Stir in maple syrup, coconut sugar, salt, and chia, flax, hemp seeds

Place oatmeal mixture in a bowl

Sprinkle banana, dried cranberries, pecans over oatmeal

Drizzle nut butter on top...and enjoy!
